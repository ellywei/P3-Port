THE HALES
BRANDING BOOK

Date: May to June 2016
Role: Branding identity and print design
Tools: Adobe Illustrator & Adobe Photoshop
Partner: Xinyue Ma

Project Intro

The Hales Branding Book is a branding package created for a branding design project in a course of Fraser International College- Graphic Design. The package is built based on an imaginary company- Hales, whose main business is producing and selling diving equipment. A set of branding design of the company was included, such as the logo, letterhead, and business card.

Process

During the first 2 weeks, we were asked to brainstorm and think about the "company" that we would like to design for and what is the style of branding should be in order to match with the "company". At this stage, my partner and I did research online on precedents, and then we did sketches on paper, finally settled down on a solution for the logo on Adobe Illustrator. The next two weeks, based on the "company" we created, different variations of the logo and other branding products like the letterhead and the business card were created, too. New to Illustrator, we watched many online tutorials to be familiar with different tools and effects in Illustrator.

Reflection

The purpose of this assignment is to get an overall idea about the process of creating a commercial branding package, including a presentation of the products- a branding book.
